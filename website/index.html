About Us

Growing Food the Natural Way

Bhoomi Natural is a pioneering natural farming initiative based in Shamli district, Uttar Pradesh, India. We practice and promote regenerative agriculture through food forest development.

Our 10-acre farm demonstrates how no-till, chemical-free farming can produce abundant harvests while nurturing the soil and supporting biodiversity. From towering fruit trees to ground-cover vegetables, our multi-layer food forests create thriving ecosystems.

Led by Abhay Rod, we help landowners across India transform their properties into productive food forests - from Mumbai hillsides to Kolkata wastelands to Noida plots.

  • No-till farming methodology
  • Multi-layer food forest design
  • Chemical-free, organic produce
  • Educational farm visits
