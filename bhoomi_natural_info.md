# Bhoomi Natural - Comprehensive Information

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

---

## Overview

Bhoomi Natural Farms is a serene food forest and natural farming operation located in Shamli district, Uttar Pradesh, India. The farm is a testament to sustainable agriculture and natural farming practices.

---

## Founder & Team

**Founder:** Shyam Singh Rod
- **Background:** Artist turned farmer, former art teacher
- **Journey:** Started natural farming in 2016 on 2 acres of inherited land
- **Expansion:** Gradually expanded to bring all 10 acres under natural farming
- **Education:** Attended a five-day training in Lucknow to learn about natural farming
- **Family:** His son Abhay Rod also works on the farm

## The Food Forest

### Concept
"I designed a food forest that would be self-sufficient and not require external inputs. We named it Bhoomi Natural Farms." - Shyam Singh Rod

### Size & Scale
- Total Area: 10 acres
- Started: 2016 with 2 acres, expanded over the years
- Over 40 varieties of crops grown

### Farming Practices
- **Method:** Natural farming (no chemicals or external inputs)
- **Philosophy:** Self-sufficient food forest ecosystem
- **Sustainability:** Chemical-free, organic approach
- **Biodiversity:** Diverse crop varieties grown together
